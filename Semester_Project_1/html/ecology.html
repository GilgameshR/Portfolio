Ecology

Eco friendly energy is a hugely important part of our world right now, and it's necessity is only increasing. This water dam outputs enough energy to power thousands of houses, and its job is only becomming more and more important as our need for electricity increases. here at the museum we exhibit a scaled version of this very dam. best of all? its fully operational, learn everything there is to know about why this energy source and it's potential for the future!

With global warming already upon us the ice around the world has started to melt, we want to help prevent this and ensure a better world for the generations to come. Join us and learn what you can do and how you can help to make this world a place we can thrive in for generations to come.

This is one of the oil rigs in the northern sea, it's been producing the oil that fuels cars for years and has been a very important part of life as we know it, these days humans have become more environmentally minded and people have begun questioning whether these oil rigs should be allowed to continue operating. Our scaled model shows you how these works, and our goal is to help you learn enough to form your own opinion, weighing both the pros and cons.

These funny looking humongus windmills are facinating pieces of technology, they harvest the power of the weather, more specifically the wind, to power your house, cellphone and even cars. they've become incredibly important over the years and have started popping up all over the world. Learn how they work and about the increadibly cool technology behind it.
