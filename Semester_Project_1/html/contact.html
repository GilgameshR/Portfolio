Contact us

Our support staff works tirelessly to make sure our visitors have the best experience possible. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to use either at the museum’s physical location or through one of the methods listed below. We would be more than happy to assist you in any way we can.

Due to the volume of calls we receive it would be greatly appreciated if you contacted us through the appropriate channel so that we may help you as quickly as possible. The relevant numbers and emails are listed below.
