Robotics and AI

Ever wanted to see a tranformer in real life? Well now you can, this 2.5 meter tall robot is fully functional and ready to go! Learn how far technology has come, it's future possibilities and challenges, and who knows, maybe you'll be the one to tackle those challenges in the future and build your very own robot?

The Robotics and AI department is now VR capable, come see what life would be like if you lived entirely in the virtual space, do everyday activities, paint, have conversation, all while being fully immersed in a completely different world. we guarantee you it's going to be an experience like none other!

Through practical experiences we aim to teach our visitors about drone safety, its capabilities and its potential. Have you ever wanted to try flying a drone on your own? This is your chance, we have an entire outside flight area dedicated to this very purpose.

The VR area is a lot more extensive than you might believe, in this exhibit we remove you from reality entirely, travel anywhere in the world, fly through the sky and breathe under water while looking for Atlantis or buried treasure. best of all? for this particular exhibit a phone is all you need, we'll help you find a world to explore, or even create your own right here with us!
