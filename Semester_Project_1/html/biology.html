Biology and medicine

This is a full-scale model of an early infirmary, in here you can see what hospitals used to be like, and even try your hand at performing a mock surgery on one of our Anna dolls. This one is a ton of fun, join us here and save a life, Anna needs you Dr.Visitor!

The biology department houses live specimens of mice, this area doubles as a minor petting ZOO, but the primary function is teaching young minds about the importance of lab mice throughout history, and why it's becomming a hot debate topic. We want to help our future leaders form their own opinions on important and impactful topics, and this is a great place to start.

Become a researcher! see what the facinating world of microbes is like through our state of the art microscopes. There are an infinite amount of micro organisms out there, who knows, maybe you'll be the one to make the next big discovery?

This is one of our anatomy dolls, they serve the purpose of teaching human anatomy, the human body is a truly facinating piece of machinery, and learning how its fueled, how its engine works and of-course how we're able to experience all the wonderful things in life is both fun and important.
